How to Use the Map:

- Use the "*Select Year" dropdown to choose a specific year to view hurricanes in that year.
- Use the "Select Hurricane" dropdown to choose a specific hurricane name to view hurricanes with that name.
- The map will display markers representing hurricanes based on your selected filters.
- Use the "Reset Filters:" button to view all the hurricane paths
- Click on a path to view detailed information about the hurricane, including the year, name, and country.

Note: The data displayed on the map is based on the selected year and hurricane name filters. You can adjust the filters to analyze different hurricanes in different years. * = Mandatory selection field
